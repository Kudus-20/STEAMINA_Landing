The Great Things You'll Do

The perfect blend of experiences to bring out the genius in every student

Hands-on STEAM

STEAM lessons will teach students the skills to build revolutionary technology and create solutions to the world's most pressing problems. At STEAMINA, students will build live size ridable electric vehicles from scratch using modular kits.

In a world evolving faster than ever, STEAM skills are no longer optional—they are essential. The future belongs to those who can think critically, solve problems, and innovate with technology. STEAMINA gives students the hands-on experience, technical skills, and entrepreneurial mindset needed to thrive in a world driven by automation, AI, and rapid technological advancements.

Explore some of the things participants will build below:

Design-thinking

With Design Thinking, students will learn to empathize with real human needs and put people at the center of innovation. They will be introduced to industry-standard frameworks and tools that enable them to approach problem-solving with confidence and execute ideas effectively.

Student will learn to:
  • Identify real-world problems by understanding user needs and challenges.
  • Generate innovative solutions using brainstorming and ideation techniques.
  • Prototype and test ideas rapidly, improving them through iterative design.
  • Apply human-centered design frameworks used by top global innovators.
  • Communicate and pitch solutions effectively, using storytelling and persuasion.

Entrepreneurship

With Entrepreneurship Training, students will develop the mindset and skills to turn ideas into scalable solutions. They will explore business fundamentals, financial literacy, and leadership principles to bring their innovations to life and make a real impact.

Student will learn to:
  • Develop a business model, understanding value creation and customer needs.
  • Master financial literacy, learning the basics of budgeting and revenue generation.
  • Turn ideas into viable ventures, exploring how to scale innovations.
  • Build confidence in leadership, teamwork, and public speaking.
  • Pitch their startup ideas effectively, crafting compelling presentations for investors and stakeholders.

Immersive Experiences

At STEAMINA, learning goes beyond the classroom. Students will engage in real-world experiences that connect their learning to industry, innovation, and creative expression. Through guest speakers, industry visits, and immersive shows, students will gain inspiration, career insights, and a deeper appreciation for innovation in action.

Students will experience:
  • Exclusive Guest Speaker Sessions with accomplished founders, inventors, and social impact leaders who share their journeys and insights.
  • Industry Visits to Revolutionary Companies, where students explore how STEM, design thinking, and entrepreneurship are applied in the real world.
  • Hands-on Exposure to Career Paths in Tech, gaining a deeper understanding of opportunities in engineering, AI, fintech, and beyond.
  • Immersive Theatre & Cultural Experiences that bring innovation and storytelling to life, reinforcing lessons learned in the classroom.
  • Direct Networking with Industry Leaders, providing students with mentorship, career guidance, and real-world connections.

Mentorship

mentorship is at the heart of the experience. Students will be guided by accomplished Ivy League mentors throughout the camp, with ongoing mentorship extending beyond the program. These mentors will help students navigate their educational journeys, inspire them to dream big, and provide real-world advice on career paths and leadership.

Students will benefit from mentorship by:
  • Receiving personalized guidance from top university students who are leaders in their fields.
  • Developing life skills such as communication, critical thinking, and leadership through one-on-one sessions.
  • Building lasting relationships with mentors who will continue to support and guide them beyond the camp.
  • Getting career insights from mentors who have successfully navigated the path to Ivy League education and impactful careers.
  • Receiving feedback and encouragement, helping students feel empowered to pursue their ideas and ambitions with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

STEAMINA is a one week immersive summer camp in Accra, Ghana, where students aged 14 - 19 engage in hands-on STEM, design thinking, and entrepreneurship training. Guided by Ivy League mentors, students learn to build, innovate, and lead through real-world projects and mentorship.
The camp is open to students aged 14-19 from across the African continent who are eager to explore STEM, leadership, and problem-solving. No prior experience is needed—just a curious mind and a passion for innovation!
📍 Location: Fiesta Royale Hotel Accra, Ghana
📅 Dates: June 16 - June 20, 2025

STEAMINA’s curriculum covers three core areas:

  • STEM & Engineering: Build real-world projects using Infento kits, robotics, and cutting-edge technology.
  • Design Thinking: Learn how to solve real-world problems through creativity, iteration, and human-centered innovation.
  • Entrepreneurship: Understand business fundamentals, pitch ideas, and explore leadership skills.

Students will also engage in mentorship, career exploration, and industry visits to gain real-world insights.

STEAMINA is led by Ivy League students and graduates from top universities like Dartmouth, Harvard, Princeton, MIT, and Yale. These mentors guide students beyond the camp, offering ongoing support and career guidance.
  • ✅ Ivy League Mentorship – Direct learning from accomplished university students.
  • ✅ Hands-On STEM & Innovation – Students build and present real-world projects.
  • ✅ Industry Exposure & Career Exploration – Visits to Ghanaian tech companies and networking with top innovators.
  • ✅ Post-Camp Mentorship – Students continue to receive guidance even after the camp.

The camp fee is $1700, covering:

  • All learning materials & project kits
  • Meals & accommodation
  • Industry visits & immersive experiences
  • Direct mentorship from Ivy League facilitators
  • Certificate of completion

Unfortunately we are unable to offer scholarships for the 2025 camp. However, we plan to offer scholarships starting next year. Please state tuned!
